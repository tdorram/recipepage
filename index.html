Two Ingredient Strawberry Donuts!

Ingredients:

  • 1 Package of Strawberry cake mix
  • 2 Cups of applesauce

Directions:

  1. Set oven to 425 degrees
  2. Empty strawberry cake mix into a medium sized bowl
  3. Measure out two cups of applesauce into same bowl
  4. Spray donut pan
  5. Pour batter evenly into pan
  6. Place in oven for 8-9 minutes
  7. When done, take out and let cool
  8. Optional: Dip in melted vanilla frosting and top with sprinkles!
